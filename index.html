Fast Access to Critical Information and Staff

Speed and clarity are key components for improving the quality of a patient`s care. This real-time mobile access means information is instantly shared without rounds of phone tag, costly miscommunications, or physically tracking people down.

Secure, Compliant Communications

With HIPAA fines rising in both frequency and dollar amount, the need to protect patient data at every stage is becoming ever more critical. A secure, encrypted, centrally managed messaging solution is one way to safeguard clinical communications.

Centralized, Integrated Communications Platform

Centralizing clinical communications and alerts on a single, integrated platform, critical information that would ordinarily be spread across multiple applications and hospital systems can now be instantly accessed by hospital staff from a smartphone or web browser

Multi Organization access and privacy

This feature lets healthcare staff register in multiple organizations and create personal groups for cross organization communication.
