We are here to help you

Document Management System is online document management software that enables organizations to automate and manage the complete document and file management cycle. Its robust functionality helps companies with managing business documents, version control, process workflows, records, and folders from a centralized location. The online document management software has been integrated with eSignature solution to simplify the approval process.

Document Management System also includes team collaboration and file management features to manage files, records, and retention & disposition schedules.

Key features

  • Document Management System
  • Now upload and access your important files and folders online
  • Access from anywhere, anytime
  • Download them to your local system
  • View Online for safe viewing
  • Multiple logins and permission restrictions for users
  • Includes 5GB storage with basic account
  • Additional space in multiples of 5GB
  • Refile your files with patient indexing
  • Track or find files easily with quick search feature
